Everything you need to know about bridges

  • A bridge is the medium that hosts the seller’s product/service details and the buyer’s payment.

  • A bridge releases the buyer’s payment to the seller after the buyer gets and verifies the product or service.

  • A bridge is created by a seller and shared to a buyer to accept so that the trade may get started.

How Bridges Work

  • Create Bridge

    Enter order details and your payment info

  • Share Bridge

    Send link of the bridge to the buyer

  • Buyer Accepts

    Buyer makes payment for the order

  • Deliver Order

    Provide the buyer's order at this stage

  • Receive Payment

    Get paid after buyer gets their order
